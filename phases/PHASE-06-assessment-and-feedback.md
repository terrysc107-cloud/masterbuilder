# Phase 06 Playbook - Assessment And Feedback

## Objective

Make the product measurable, coachable, or self-improving through checkpoints and feedback.

## Lead And Support Agents

- Lead: `Assessment Builder`
- Support: `Content Systems Builder`, `Frontend Builder`, `Verifier`

## Scout Audit Questions

1. How does the product currently measure progress or quality?
2. What is missing from the feedback loop?
3. Which checkpoints are weak or absent?
4. Are users learning from results or only seeing scores?
5. What signals should shape next-step recommendations?

## Required Outputs

- meaningful checkpoints
- clearer explanations and feedback
- stronger progression or quality signals
- improved recommendation inputs where appropriate

## Exit Criteria

- users can tell how well they are doing
- the product reinforces understanding and improvement
- feedback loops feel purposeful

## Execution Prompt

```text
Execute Phase 06 using docs/MASTER-PLAN.md and this playbook.
Adopt the roles of Assessment Builder, Content Systems Builder, Frontend Builder, Verifier, and Scout.
Audit the current feedback layer first, then make it genuinely useful.
```
