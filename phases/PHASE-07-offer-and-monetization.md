# Phase 07 Playbook - Offer And Monetization

## Objective

Connect the product to pricing, premium value, and service paths without damaging trust.

## Lead And Support Agents

- Lead: `Offer Strategist`
- Support: `Flow Architect`, `Frontend Builder`, `Design Auditor`

## Scout Audit Questions

1. Is the business model visible in the product?
2. Which parts should feel free, premium, or service-led?
3. Are upgrade points clear and honest?
4. Does the offer feel integrated into the experience?
5. What should wait until backend entitlements are real?

## Required Outputs

- clearer offer structure
- stronger premium or service messaging
- more honest upgrade paths
- better product-business alignment

## Exit Criteria

- monetization feels native rather than bolted on
- users can understand the value ladder
- trust remains intact

## Execution Prompt

```text
Execute Phase 07 using docs/MASTER-PLAN.md and this playbook.
Adopt the roles of Offer Strategist, Flow Architect, Frontend Builder, Design Auditor, and Scout.
Audit the current product-business connection first, then strengthen it without manipulative tactics.
```
