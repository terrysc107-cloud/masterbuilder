# Phase 11 Playbook - Launch Readiness

## Objective

Make the product coherent, credible, and ready to be shared or sold.

## Lead And Support Agents

- Lead: `Chief Builder`
- Support: `Verifier`, `Offer Strategist`, `Flow Architect`, `Learning Steward`

## Scout Audit Questions

1. Is the main product journey strong enough?
2. Is the offer clear enough?
3. Is the design polished enough to build trust?
4. Is the product easy to demo and explain?
5. What final debt still blocks confidence?

## Required Outputs

- final readiness review
- clear launch blockers or approval
- final trust and coherence pass
- learning update for future projects

## Exit Criteria

- the product can be shared publicly without apology
- the experience feels coherent and intentional
- the team can explain what the product does and why it matters

## Execution Prompt

```text
Execute Phase 11 using docs/MASTER-PLAN.md and this playbook.
Adopt the roles of Chief Builder, Verifier, Offer Strategist, Flow Architect, Learning Steward, and Scout.
Audit the full product honestly first, then close the real launch blockers only.
```
